An actor driven ensemble based in NYC

Born in the space between formal acting training and established careers, Battalion recognizes and revels in the wealth of talent to be found in emerging artists. Our founders believe in utilizing the full scope and diversity of interests and skills to produce lively, joyful, significant evenings of theater, consciously growing with each production. With varying backgrounds in classical, modern, and experimental theater Battalion serves as an incredibly flexible vehicle for expression, simultaneously celebrating its strengths and offering the challenge of something new.

About

The holidays have driven us a little batty. Thus we offer you 'An All About Eve-ning'; a slightly staged reading of one of the greatest American movies of all time. Watch, or listen rather, as Eve Harrington-an ambitious young fan- tries to work her way into the life of her idol, Margo Channing-a Broadway star.


We're talkin' hunger! Manipulation! Desparation! Sex! Fur coats! The only movie in history to receive four acting nominations for women! And lots of martinis!!


Short on cash? Excellent! This is a pay-what-you-want after the performance situation and comes with free cookies, drinks, and other holiday treats!
